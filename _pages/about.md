I am an M.Phil. student in Computer and Information Engineering at [The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/en), supervised by Prof. Fangxun Zhong in the Intelligent Healthcare & Autonomous Robotics Lab.

My research focuses on robot planning and control, particularly the integration of model-based control and embodied learning for robust and reliable robotic manipulation, locomotion, and autonomous systems.

Research Interests
======

* Robot planning and control
* Robot manipulation and locomotion
* Embodied intelligence

Current Work
======

My primary current project is vision-guided robotic nasal endoscope control and safety supervision, aiming to help surgeons perform nasal surgery with stable, constrained, and safe robotic endoscope motion.

For this project, my technical focus is on visual servoing, RCM constraints, MPC, and multi-task robot planning for robot-assisted nasal endoscopy.

Education
======

* **M.Phil. in Computer and Information Engineering**, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Sep 2025 - expected Jun 2027
* **B.E. in Robotics**, Northwestern Polytechnical University, Sep 2021 - Jun 2025
